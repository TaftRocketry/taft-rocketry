OUR MISSION

Welcome to Taft High Rocketry (THR), where we're pioneering the future of aerospace technology. Inspired by JPL's innovative spirit, we're a team of passionate students dedicated to pushing the boundaries of rocketry and space exploration.


Founded in 2025, THR has quickly established itself as a leader in student rocketry, combining cutting-edge engineering with bold experimentation.


We design, build, and launch high-power rockets capable of reaching altitudes of over 5,000 feet. Our work utilizes advanced propulsion systems, custom avionics, and innovative recovery solutions.


Join us as we work towards our ultimate goal: being the first valley high school team to launch a rocket!

Green Goblin

4,000 ft H-Class Motor Custom Avionics

Our primary high-power rocket featuring parachute recovery and advanced telemetry systems.

Technical Specifications

  • Height: 4 ft
  • Diameter: 2.3 in
  • Weight: 1.2 kg
  • Motor: Homemade
