TAFT ROCKETRY

Who We Are

Welcome to the official website of Taft High Rocketry! We are a student-led rocketry team based at Taft High School in Los Angeles, California. Our mission is to design, build, and launch high-power rockets while fostering a passion for STEM and aerospace engineering among our members.
